> [!example]+ Five Essential Verbs in dplyr
> - `filter()`
>     - Select subset of rows (observations)
> - `arrange()`
>     - Reorder rows
> - `select()`
>     - Select subset of columns (variables)
> - `mutate()`
>     - Create new columns that are functions of existing columns
> - `summarize()`
>     - Collapse data into a single row
