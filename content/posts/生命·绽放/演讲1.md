> [!abstract] Talk about managing a budget.
> - Why is it important to stick to a budget?
> - Who should have a budget?  
> - Do you personally have a budget?  

Managing a budget is important because it helps you <u>control your spending, save for future needs, and avoid unnecessary debt</u>.   
By tracking your income and expenses, you can make sure you’re <mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">living within your means and planning for both short-term and long-term goals</mark>.   

Everyone should have a budget—students, working adults, and even families.   
For students, a budget helps manage school expenses and daily costs.   
For adults, it can support saving for a house, a car, or retirement.   

I personally have a simple budget that I update each month.   
I list my income, like part-time job earnings, and then plan how much to spend on food, transport, school supplies, and savings.   
It helps me avoid spending too much on non-essentials and reminds me to save for important things.   
Having a budget gives me peace of mind and helps me feel more in control of my finances. It’s a useful habit that benefits anyone.

- **living within your means**：根据自己的收入水平安排开支

> [!abstract] Talk about why decorations matter for social gatherings.
> - Do decorations help set the mood for events? Why or why not?
> - Are they necessary for a successful gathering? Why or why not?  

Decorations play an important role in social gatherings because they help <mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">set the mood</mark> and make the event feel special.   
For example, soft lights and flowers can create a warm, welcoming feeling at a wedding, while bright colors and balloons bring energy and fun to a birthday party. Decorations can match the theme of the event and make guests feel more excited and involved. 

While decorations are not always necessary for a successful gathering, they do add to the experience. 
A gathering can still be enjoyable with good food and friendly people, but decorations show thought and effort, <mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">which</mark> guests often appreciate. 
They also make the space more attractive and help create <mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">lasting memories</mark>, especially in photos. 
In short, decorations aren’t required, but they can improve the atmosphere and make any event more <mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">festive and meaningful</mark>. 
Whether simple or fancy, the right decorations help people feel more connected and happy during social celebrations.  

- set the mood 营造氛围
