# Course Description
With the large amount of data available for businesses and organizations from various sources, staying competitive requires leveraging business analytics to make informed decisions. This course is designed to equip students with the essential skills in data visualization and decision analytics. Through this course, students will gain a clear understanding of how to use analytics to identify business problems and provide solutions and recommendations. This course will not only cover theoretical knowledge but also practical skills using real-world applications and practical exercises. The course will provide hands-on experience in visualizing and analyzing data as well as making quantitative decisions, preparing students to meet the demands of today's job market, and making them valuable assets to future employers.

> [!info] Userful Reading Materials:
> - **Business Analytics for Managers**, by Wolfgang Jank, Springer 2011 (free online access through UD library)  
> - **Tableau Your Data!**, by Daniel Murray, Wiley 2016  
> - **Storytelling with Data**, Cole Nussbaumer Knaflic, Wiley 2015
